Rooms But Significantly Worse

Rooms But Significantly Worse is a satire of the numerous Rooms clones that showed up on Roblox after Doors, a game inspired by rooms, became popular. These Rooms clones usually tried to remaster or build upon the original Rooms often were low in quality. Rooms But Significantly Worse leans more into comedy than horror, unlike its predecessors.

Find it on: Roblox
